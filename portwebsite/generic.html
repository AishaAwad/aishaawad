Aisha Awad

"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like,
but about creating meaningful experiences that solve
and improve the lives of users."

Hi there! My name is Aisha and I am a UX designer. I have a strong passion for design and user experience, and I believe that good design has the power to greatly improve the lives of users. I love to use my creativity and problem-solving skills to create intuitive and user-friendly designs that meet the needs of users.

I am constantly learning and staying up-to-date with the latest design trends and best practices in order to create designs that not only look good, but work seamlessly for the user. I have a strong foundation in design principles and have completed coursework in UX design and have gained valuable skills through personal projects and online resources. I am committed to continuously learning and improving my abilities as a designer.
